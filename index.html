About us

National Institute of science Education and Research is one of the pre-eminent research institutes of our country established with a mandate and vision to be the thought engine of the growth for our country. The institute has come a long way from its dawn and has grown with fortitude form a puisne to a stalwart. The institute has consistently produced studetns who are trained and educated in forefront sciences and technologies with an industrious mindset. Udbhava, the cultural and science fest of NISER is back, charged and reloaded with an aim to usher the youthful colors of joy to the society and assimilate the scientific thinking with the rich culture of the temple city of india, Bhubaneshwar. This fest will give you an opportunity to bathe yourself in the spirit of happiness and sacrosanct knowledge. The fest intends to bring a positive change in society and develop it to be a respected institution which is know for its power through its limitations, is decisive through its diplomacy and is respected in its humility.
