    About us

    National Institute of Science Education and Research is one of the premier research institutes of our country established with a mandate and vision to be the thought engine of the growth for our country. The institute has come a long way from its dawn and has grown with fortitude. The institute has consistently produced supremely talented students who are educated in diverse disciplines of the sciences. Udbhava, the cultural and science fest of NISER is back, charged and reloaded, with an aim to usher in the colours of vibrancy to society and assimilate the scientific thinking with the rich culture of the temple city of India, Bhubaneswar. This fest gives you an opportunity to bask in the amalgation of spirited youth, and passion of science. The fest intends to bring a positive change in society, infusing in it the qualities our institution boasts of, delivering despite limitations, uncovering stalwarts of various disciplines. The fest is a reflection of the institute in all its glory, known for its understated achievements, picturesque landscapes, respected for its humility and ploughing on through shortcomings. We hope that every person who is a part of this humble dedication, is witness to and recognises the institute, built with its foundation of knowledge and curiosity, standing upright with the strength of its patrons.
